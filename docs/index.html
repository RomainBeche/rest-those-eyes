FAQ

A small FAQ is usually enough for App Store support, especially when the app is simple and self-explanatory.

How do I change the break interval?

Open the app from the menu bar and adjust the work interval in settings. You can choose anything from 10 to 60 minutes.

Can I snooze a break?

Yes. When a reminder appears, you can snooze it for a short period and return to it when the current task is finished.

Why am I not seeing notifications?

Check macOS notification permissions for the app in System Settings. If notifications are disabled there, reminders cannot appear.

Does the app send any data online?

No. The app works locally on your Mac and does not rely on a remote account or backend.
